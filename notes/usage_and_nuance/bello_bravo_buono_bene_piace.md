# bello_bravo_buono_bene_piace

Level: A2
Category: Usage & Nuance

Purpose:
Drill the nuance between **bello**, **bravo**, **buono**, **bene**, and **piacere**.

## Rules
bello: appearance / emotional evaluation  
bravo: skill  
buono: moral goodness / quality  
bene: verb performance (adverb)

### Adjective position
Some adjectives change meaning depending on position.

#### BEFORE the noun
- evaluative / “what kind of X?”
- often = great / admirable

una bella persona = a wonderful person / un bel cuoco = a great cook / un grande uomo = a great man

### AFTER the noun
- descriptive / literal property

una persona bella = an attractive person / un cuoco bello = a good-looking cook / un uomo grande = a big man
